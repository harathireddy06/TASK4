Our Story

Mission & Vision

Founded in 2015, GreenFootprint began with a simple belief: that every individual has the power to heal the planet. What started as a local initiative has grown into a global movement of environmental stewards.

Mission

To facilitate large-scale reforestation and empower communities with the tools for long-term ecological sustainability.

Vision

A world where humanity lives in perfect harmony with nature, ensuring a green legacy for generations to come.

Our Journey

2015

First 1,000 trees planted in Mumbai.

2018

Reached 10,000 volunteers milestone.

2021

Launched National Sustainability Program.

2024

Awarded Green Excellence Global Prize.
